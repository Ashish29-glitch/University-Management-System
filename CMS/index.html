About Parala Maharaja Engineering College

Parala Maharaja Engineering College (PMEC), Berhampur, came into existence in the year 2009. It is a constituent Government Engineering College of Biju Patnaik University of Technology (BPUT), Odisha. This college is one of the premier technical institutes in southern Odisha.

The main objective of the institution is to produce quality technical manpower.
